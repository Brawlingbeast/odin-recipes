Homemade Pizza Dough Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 3 to 4 cups of all-purpose flour(if possible try and find flour with the ingredients "Whole wheat" and only "Whole Wheat") My choice of flour
  2. 2 Tbsp of olive oil
  3. 1/2 cup of Oregano
  4. 1/4 cup of Sumac
  5. 1/4 cup of Tumeric
  6. 1 pack of pizza yeast or equivalent
  7. 1 1/2 cup of warm water

Incase you wondering "where the sugar?" This recipe does not require it. Some chefs would recommend sugar for the yeast, but I've noticed that what yeast requires is light and/or heat. Feel free to try this at home when creating the dough and put it in the sunlight to watch it grow.

Steps on how to make the dough

  • Step 1: Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl
  • Step 2: Add the water and oil to the bowl.
  • Step 3: Meed the dough thoroughly by hand or by using a machine, until the dough until it retakes its' form. It should act a bit like playdough
  • Step 3.5: While mixing the dough you might need to add water and/or flour. Do so in increments of 1 Tbsp. Mix between each addition.
  • Step 4: Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or reusable cover. Then place it under a light or (if possible) the light. The yeast grows better in light.
